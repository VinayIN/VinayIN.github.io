Who am I ?

My academics/personal project stuffs can be found here @GITHUB. If you want to collaborate on any projects of yours feel free to ping me anytime.

I'm originally from a remote village in the state of Jharkhand, India and currently residing in Bangalore, India. I completed my undergraduation in '19 from IIIT Bhubaneswar majoring in IT under CS department. Now, I work as a Trainee - Data Science at Utopia Labs, Bangalore developing few data cleansing products and maintaining them for the team.


I enjoy participating in Hackathons, programming and exploring the state of AI in deep learning, natural language processing and computer vision. I've spent '18 summer as a research intern at IDRBT, Hyderabad. After which I did an internship with Utopia Labs, Bangalore for my spring term before being a full time employee.


In my spare time I like to travel a lot, play sports preferably outdoor, watch movies and do theatre. I like keeping myself fit. If you meet me feel free to converse about fitness, movies, adventures and of course, coding.
