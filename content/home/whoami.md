> Machine Learning Engineer | AI Engineer | Data Scientist

Namaste, I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at the [Brandenburg University of Technology Cottbus-Senftenberg (BTU)](https://www.b-tu.de/).

I transitioned from the professional sphere to pursue my Master's degree, captivated by the current surge in academia and to engage deeply within diverse research areas. I am a strong advocate for [ethical applications of AI](https://www.iso.org/artificial-intelligence/responsible-ai-ethics) and believe in its positive implications to society. Currently, I am based in [Cottbus, Germany](https://www.cottbus.de/kultur/index.html), writing my masters thesis on model compression strategy.

My academic and professional interests are `system design`, `speech recognition models` *(ASR)*, `model compression` *(pruning and quantization)*, and application of `reinforcement learning` approaches for operations optimization.
